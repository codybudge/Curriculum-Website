Real-Life Object

A large part of any 3D modelling is being able to create real-world objects. Whether its a light fixture, a desk, or a dish, these small details are used for everything from video games and animated movies, to product databases.

Your task is to recreate something from Lab using SketchUp. Be careful to choose an object that has the type of geometry you know how to create already (avoid very curvy objects).

Step 1

Choose an object and check with your instructor that it will be useable.

Step 2

Build as exact a replica of that object as possible, matching the shape and materials as closely as you can.

Complete this task and inform your instructor of your success

(or to ask for help. This isn't a quiz, after all).
