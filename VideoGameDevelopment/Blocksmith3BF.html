Blocksmith 3

BOSS FIGHT

15 points

Big Bad Boss Fight

Just when you think that they've been beaten, the toughest bosses power-up and double-down on their efforts to beat you! Now it's time for you to mastermind the biggest, baddest boss fight that you've attempted yet...

Use one of your multi-level games, or create a new one, and add a boss fight that takes multiple scenes to defeat. Each scene of the bossfight must get harder than the last, and end with a finale that's as epic as you can create.

Step 1

Load or create a new multi-level game, with all the features and requirements of the one made in Blocksmith 2.

Step 2

Add a new boss at the end of the last level. Make this bosses scene send the player to a second bossfight where the boss is more difficult to defeat.

Step 3

Create a 3rd and final phase of the boss fight where the boss is even more difficult. Include other dangers in the scene that will challenge the player in addition to the boss.

Step 4

Include a win screen, and make each phase of the boss fight act like a checkpoint, so the player doesn't restart from the beginning if they lose.

Complete this task and inform your instructor of your success

(or to ask for help. This isn't a quiz, after all).
