UNREAL 1 - Intro to the Editor

Step 1

OPEN UNREAL ENGINE

Open "Epic Games Launcher" from the desktop. When the launcher opens, select "Unreal Engine" at the top, and then select "Launch".


Don’t go crazy with the clicking if it doesn’t immediately open. Since Unreal Engine is a heavy-duty software, the loading time might take a few minutes. When you see the “Launch” button change to “Launching” you can move on to the next steps below. Plus one for multitasking!

While Unreal Engine is loading, go grab yourself a pair of headphones. Then, open the video below to begin your quest. Good luck, and remember to ask for help from your instructors at any time!

Step 2

FOLLOW ALONG IN YOUR OWN PROJECT

Play the video below, and follow along with the instructions and practice – you’ll need to know this stuff to make your game later! Quick tip – you can switch between your project in Unreal Engine and the Video by pressing ALT+TAB. A good idea is to watch a bit of the video and then pause it. When its paused, press ALT+TAB to see your project. Practice what you saw, then press ALT+TAB again to go back and continue the video (another one for multitasking!).
