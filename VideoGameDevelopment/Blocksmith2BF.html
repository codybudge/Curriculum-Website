A Mile in Their Shoes

Have you ever thought about what it would be like to be an ant for a day? What about a fish, or a mountain goat, sewer rat, or chicken!? Each one of these very different creatures faces a variety of different dangers in their day-to-day lives. Imagine what it would be like to face the same kinds of situations that they face. With VR, now you can bring that imaginary experience to life!

Choose a creature of some sort and create an experience that encompasses all (or more) of what that creature might be exposed to in your own multi-level game. The creature should have some endgoal that is appropriate for what it is (e.g. a beetle might traverse obstacles to get to a pile of gargbage, and a goat might want to get to a nice pasture of clovers).

Step 1

Choose a creature and make the player settings simulate its movement capabilities

Step 2

Create at least 3 levels with obstacles, traps, enemies, projectiles, and other various dangers

Step 3

Make an ending scene with animations that shows the player the achieved goal for your creature

Step 4

Add a scene that conveys the demise of the player (a losing screen), but also gives the player a chance to try again.

Complete this task and inform your instructor of your success

(or to ask for help. This isn't a quiz, after all).
