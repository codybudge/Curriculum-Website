BLOCKSMITH 4

BOSS FIGHT

25 points

The Endgame

You and your friends are walking through a snowy forest. Out of nowhere a Yeti attacks - The only way to beat it is to team up with your friends in the highest-stakes snowball fight of your life! This is just an example of the kind of creative experience you're free to design ahead. You've come a long way, and defeated more than your fair share of bosses, and you've reached the pinnacle of your Blocksmith adventure. It's time to team-up with your friends to take down the final boss.

Make a multiplayer game with a multi-level boss fight that requires multiple successful attacks to progress through the boss fight phases. Teamwork is the key in this adventure, and you will need to include that as a key element in the success of your players.

Step 1

Create an opening scene(or multiple scenes) that set up the final boss fight with an impressive animation or series of animations.

Step 2

Create a multiplayer boss fight that includes the player's ability to use props in the player slots, and to interact with objects in the scene.

Step 3

The boss must take multiple attacks to be "defeated" and move to the next phase. Each phase should require more successful attacks, or require more difficult actions of the player to defeat the boss.

Complete this task and inform your instructor of your success

(or to ask for help. This isn't a quiz, after all).
