Blocksmith 1 - Intro to Blocksmith

Step 1

Set Up an Account

Open the Blocksmith Path from the computer's desktop, and then open both the Blocksmith Builder and the Blocksmith Website. From the website. With the website open, create your own account by following the steps below.

When your request is submitted, close the tab. Make sure that an instructor knows so that they can grant you access to the domain. You'll need to have an account in order to continue.

Step 2

Start your quest

When you are granted access to the domain, you are ready to go on to the next task. There are three tasks that you need to complete in order to attempt the boss fight, available below. These three tasks must be completed in order, because they build on the knowledge that you learn in the previous one. When each one is completed, submit the project through your online account.

1. Shape a Castle

2. Make a Spaceship

3. Remake Roswell

Submitting Projects

Through the Blocksmith website
