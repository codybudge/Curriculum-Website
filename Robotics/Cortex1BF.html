Enter the Robot

Many things in day to day life are robotic. Hot-tubs, Microwaves, Vacuums - things that we might never have considered a "robot" but that complete automated tasks for us. Your task is to create a robotic gateway that raises and lowers an arm.

Step 1

Build a motorized arm and platform that will hold the arm parallel to the ground.

Step 2

Program the robotic arm to raise and lower, as if it were letting traffic through.

Complete this task and inform your instructor of your success

(or to ask for help. This isn't a quiz, after all).
